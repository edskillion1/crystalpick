Crystal Collector

How you play the game


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Match"
  2. There are four crystals below.
    • Green
    • Yellow
    • Blue
    • Red
  3. Clicking on a crystal adds a certain amount of points to your total score.
  4. Match your total score to the random number and you WIN.
  5. If your total score is higher than the random number you LOSE.
  6. Each of the crystals value is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. When the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
